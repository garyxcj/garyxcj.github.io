CS Ph.D. Candidate · University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

I am a fourth-year CS Ph.D. candidate at UIUC, advised by Prof. Bo Li. I received my B.S. in CS from Zhejiang University.

My research makes AI systems safe, scalable, and interpretable. I work on red-teaming that uncovers realistic agent failures, interpretability analysis of reasoning and hallucinations, and efficient post-training for reliable deployment.
